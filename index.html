Features

Weapons

Choose carefully your starting weapon between 3 starters.
You can after choose in a wide variety of weapons with different gameplay. You can upgrade your weapon to gain different bonuses.


Armors

When finishing a room, you can take an armor. Depending on the armor you have equipped, you can have differents effects applied to your stats, such as a speed boost.





Artifacts

When finishing a room, you can choose an artifact applying different effects to the player or the enemy.
If another chest offers you the same artifact, you can upgrade their effect! Choose strategically to survive the longer you can!
